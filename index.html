Hello there!
I am Aiman Abdel Samad, frontend developer from Bogotá (with egiptian roots). Graphic Designer from the Universidad Nacional de Colombia, and I currently study Engineering Informatics at the Universidad Santo Tomás.

I work as frontend developer full-time since 2013. Previously, graphic designer (interactive and programmer in Flash for nine years). I occasionally work in UX/UI, branding, typographic, editorial and infographic design.

I love read (classics, sciences, sci-fi, philosophy, art, history, biography... check out my Goodreads page) and cats. I'm passionate about mathematics, physics, astronomy, cosmology, watercolour painting and classical piano (I've spending years studying only one Bach piece).
